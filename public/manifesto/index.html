The launch manifesto




“After we stopped going to the moon, it all ended. We stopped dreaming.” –Neil deGrasse Tyson



We started out with Moonshot Academy with our mind towards the future. A better future. A future that can only be built with the help of the creators, the doers, and the makers emerging from science and engineering schools all over the planet. We believe that the future is that of enabling technologies, allowing us to explore from the deepest of space to the smallest cells of our bodies. With Moonshot Academy we aim to inspire the students of today and bring them closer to STEM education.

We want more people to understand, learn and even experiment with the basics of sciences and technology. We want to motivate the next generations to go to Mars or establish a permanent settlement on the Moon, to build space-ships in orbit or to repair a human body from inside-out. We want them to build tomorrow, the science-fiction of today.


This daring vision has to be grounded in actionable plans, focusing our ideas in concrete activities. We concentrated all our thoughts and will dedicate all our efforts in fulfilling one mantra:


“Science and technology made easy.”

With this mantra in mind, we are on our way to change the worldwide perception of science and technology as a career choice. We are confident that we will be able to show everybody the engaging and interesting side of science. The side of science that creates passion for knowledge, the passion for creating and discovering, the passion that drives every engineer or scientist to achieve more and tackle bigger challenges. We want them to think of a better and brighter future, empowered by the advanced technologies they will create!


We want them to be able to dream!
